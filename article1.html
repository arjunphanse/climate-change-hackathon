Flooding Simulator

We have created a Flooding Simulator which simulates the rising sea levels of coastal cities like New York City (NY), Miami (FL), Galveston (TX), etc. In this simulator, the sea levels are predicted to rise this century (2020 to 2100), and the magnitide of the sea level rise is determined by the climate policies we pursue and the carbon dioxide extraction policies we implement. These policies can be set using the button at the top of the simluator and their changing impact on sea levels is predicted.
